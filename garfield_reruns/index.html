Ever wondered how cartoonists come up with something new every single day? Well, sometimes they don’t. Here are some examples of Jim Davis’s Garfield that are reminiscent of past strips. I’ve sorted them by ascending similarity. You can start with the ones that are simply very close variations on a theme, skip to strips that repeat a gag, or jump down to the most identical duplicates. Enjoy!
