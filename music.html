Vämod's Tale
Vämod's Tale is a song by melodic death metal band King of Asgard. Its lyrics are an English translation of the Rök Runestone, an ancient Swedish runestone carved c. 900 AD about events c. 600 AD and found in a wall of a church in the 19th century.
